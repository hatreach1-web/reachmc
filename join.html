How to Join

Step 1: Open Minecraft

Launch your Minecraft client. We support versions 1.16 through the latest!

Step 2: Go to Multiplayer

Click on "Multiplayer" and then click "Add Server" at the bottom.

Step 3: Enter the IP

Set the Server Name to ReachMC and type in our Server IP below. Then click "Done".

play.reachmc.fun

Step 4: Join and Survive!

Double click our server in your list to join. Don't forget to join the Discord to claim your invite rewards!
